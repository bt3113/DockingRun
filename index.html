Drone Run

Chase the drone through narrow corn rows. Collect signal pings, dodge debris, and use boost before time runs out.

How to Play

  • Drag left or right to steer.
  • Collect blue pings to gain time and boost.
  • Avoid hay bales, rocks, and broken fence posts.
  • Hold boost when the meter has charge.
